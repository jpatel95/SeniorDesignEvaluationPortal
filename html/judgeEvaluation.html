Senior Design Evaluation

Evaluation Criteria
Please evaluate design projects and presentations using the following point system:

5 = Excellent (at the level of an entry-level engineer you would hire)

4 = Good (at the level of an accomplished college senior)

3 = Average (at the level of a typical college senior)

2 = Below Average (not up to the expectations of a college senior)

1 = Poor (significant errors or omissions)

N/A = If no appropriate score applies
